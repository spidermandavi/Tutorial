Final-Rank-Elite Payout Rules

1. Leader Cut

The leader keeps 20% of each tournament prize. The remaining 80% is split among players.

2. Tournament Types

  • ChessMood – monthly tournaments
  • Streamers – monthly tournaments

3. Top Player Tiers

The 80% player pool is split into three tiers:

  • Top 20 → 50% of player pool
  • Next 30 → 30% of player pool
  • Next 50 → 20% of player pool

4. Eligibility

To receive payout from a tournament, a player must:

  • Play at least 10 games (wins + losses + draws)
  • Have a win rate ≥ 20% (wins / (wins + losses); draws ignored)
  • Not have had their earnings manually cleared

5. Score-Based Payout

Earnings are distributed proportionally to each player's score (points) within their tier. If a player is ineligible, their share is redistributed inside their bracket. If there are fewer than 100 participants, missing slots go to the leader.

6. Minimum Payout

Players must accumulate at least 5€ before receiving payment. Amounts below this remain recorded and accumulate for future tournaments.

7. Transaction Fees

Transaction fees apply and are deducted from payouts. The site does not calculate fees automatically.

8. Payment Methods

Payments are made via PayPal. Other methods may be possible but are not guaranteed. Some countries may not be eligible.
