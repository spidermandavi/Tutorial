Final-Rank-Elite Payout Rules

1. Leader Cut

The leader keeps 20% of each tournament prize. The remaining 80% is split among the players.

2. Tournament Types

  • ChessMood – monthly tournaments, prize depends on normal/December rules
  • Streamers – monthly tournaments with fixed prize table

3. Top Player Tiers

The remaining prize pool is split into three tiers:

  • Top 20 → 50% of player pool
  • Next 30 → 30% of player pool
  • Next 50 → 20% of player pool

4. Dynamic Adjustment

If fewer than 100 players participate:

  • The last tier (51–100) is split as if 50 players exist; leftover goes to the leader.
  • If ≥100 participants, all tiers are fully distributed.

5. Net Wins & Debt Carryover

Each player earns money only for net positive wins per tournament type. Draws do not count. Negative netWins are carried over until the player first becomes positive.

6. Win Rate Tiebreaker

Players with the same score in a tier are compared by win rate (wins / (wins + losses)), ignoring draws.

7. Reset / Liquidation

The admin can reset a player’s earnings for a specific type, leaving their historical stats intact. This is used to pay out players.

9. Notes

  • Calculations are done separately for ChessMood and Streamers tournaments.
  • History of netWins, scores, and rankings is always kept.
  • Leaderboard shows Rank, Net Wins, Win Rate, Earnings per type, Total Earnings.
